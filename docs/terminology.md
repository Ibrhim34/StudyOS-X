# StudyOS X Terminology

## Chapter

A major learning unit composed of multiple topics.

---

## Topic

A single concept or learning objective explained independently.

---

## Learning Blueprint

The optimized teaching sequence produced by the Learning Planner.

---

## Knowledge Graph

The structured representation of concepts and their relationships.

---

## Component

A single module with one responsibility inside the StudyOS X Engine.

---

## Standard

A document defining mandatory rules followed by all components.

---

## Contract

A formal definition of inputs, outputs and responsibilities between components.

---

## Engine

The complete pipeline responsible for transforming educational material into a structured learning experience.
